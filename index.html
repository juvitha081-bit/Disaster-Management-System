  • Fire: 101
  • Ambulance: 102
  • Police: 100
  • Disaster Helpline: 108

Flood Safety

  • Stay updated with weather forecasts and warning messages.
  • Avoid walking or driving through flooded roads.
  • Move to higher ground immediately when water levels rise.
  • Keep an emergency kit with water, food, medicines, flashlight, and important documents.
  • Turn off electricity and gas supply if water enters the house.
  • Follow evacuation instructions from authorities.

Earthquake Safety

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On under a sturdy table or desk.
  • Stay away from windows, mirrors, and heavy objects.
  • Do not use elevators during an earthquake.
  • If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and power lines.
  • After the shaking stops, carefully exit the building and check for injuries.
  • Be prepared for aftershocks.

Fire Safety

  • Install smoke alarms and fire extinguishers in buildings.
  • Do not overload electrical sockets.
  • Keep flammable materials away from heat sources.
  • In case of fire, stay low to avoid smoke while escaping.
  • Do not use elevators during a fire.
  • Call emergency services immediately and evacuate the building
